**Description:**
Verify that the author can edit their own news and the changes are saved.

**Preconditions:**

- Author is logged in.
- News post exists.

**Steps:**

- Open the news post.
- Click the "Edit news" button.
- Modify title, content, and tags.
- Click "Submit".
- Reload or open the news post.

**Expected Result:**

- News is updated with the new values.
- Original creation date remains unchanged.

Automated Verification:

- Assert that title/content/tags are updated.
- Assert that the "created date" has not changed.\n\n### Playwright Locators Discovered (GreenCity Create News Page)
